How to play Tic Tac Tie?

Step 1: Place the Green shape on one of the 9 places that you prefer on the grid.

Step 2: Let the second player place his Shape on the grid piece he likes.

Continue until one of you has a row of 3 of your Shape or until al 9 spots are full.

Good luck and have fun!
